the drop game

PLAY

In Get The Drop game you have to chase the drop and increse your score, you can move your character by pressing arrow keys. up arrow to move in up direction down arrow to move in down direction, and for left and right, press left arrow key and right arrow key. you will get only 1 minute for play, so do your best and make maximun score.
